Check-in decisions
Condition-specific questions. Fatigue, night sweats, itching, bone pain, brain fog — tuned to ET/PV/MF symptoms. Not a generic wellness survey.
Language anchors the scale. "Quite significant — affecting daily life" replaces abstract numbers. The slider speaks in human terms.

Pattern flagging. A 28%+ increase in overall symptom load triggers a flag in the next appointment brief automatically.

Screen 10 — Our Questions
Why this changes appointments
Observations vs. questions — separated. Pre-synthesized patterns for the doctor. Specific, answerable questions for us. Not "I've been tired" — "My fatigue doubled over 8 weeks. Is this my medication or disease activity?"
Shareable before we arrive. The brief exports to MyChart. The nurse can pre-answer some questions. Appointment time is spent on what actually matters.
