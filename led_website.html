Our Story

Built by a patient.
For all of us.

I've been living with ET since 2023. For years before that, my platelets were quietly climbing in bloodwork nobody flagged. I found out I had a blood cancer thirty months after it likely caused my heart attack.

About a year into managing it, I spent sixty days living under a cloud of fatigue I couldn't explain. The data was right there on my lab printout. I just didn't have a way to understand it.

I had the data. I just didn't have a way to understand it.

My father fought colon cancer in his sixties. He had months of quiet after treatment — months of appointments and bloodwork and watching. I've wondered since whether his body was trying to tell him something that nobody, including him, had a way to hear.

That wondering is what Live Every Day is for.

Who It's For

There are millions of us managing cancer between appointments.
Most of us have never seen an app that knows our name.

MPN · Launching first
Essential Thrombocythemia, Polycythemia Vera & Myelofibrosis
Where Live Every Day begins. Built around the MPN-10 symptom instrument, CBC trend monitoring, and the specific questions MPN patients bring to their hematologist — but never have time to ask.
CLL · Coming soon
Chronic Lymphocytic Leukemia & Indolent Lymphoma
Watch and wait can last years. The monitoring never stops, the uncertainty never fully lifts. Live Every Day tracks the full picture — so when something changes, we see it in context.
Prostate · Coming soon
Prostate Cancer — Active Surveillance
Scheduled PSA tests, periodic biopsies, and the ongoing discipline of watching a diagnosis that may never require intervention. Show up to every monitoring appointment with the full longitudinal picture.
Post-treatment · Coming soon
After Treatment — The Watchful Window
Treatment is over. We've been told the best possible news. But the work of understanding our own health isn't finished — it enters a new phase. The months of close monitoring deserve the same tools as any chronic condition.
Thyroid · Coming soon
Thyroid Cancer & Early-Stage Breast Cancer
High survival rates don't mean low attention requirements. Years of hormone management, regular scans, and the ongoing discipline of long-term monitoring deserve the same quality of insight.
Expanding
More conditions on the roadmap
Each condition requires its own validated symptom instrument and specialist clinical advisor. We're building condition by condition — with the depth each one deserves.

The conditions are different. The treatments are different. The timelines are different.

But the experience is the same: bodies that don't pause between appointments, a healthcare system that can only listen a few times a year, and all of us who deserve to understand our own story — not just at the next visit, but every day.

Built Different

Built by a patient who needed it.
For all of us who do.

Independent. Disease-specific. Longitudinal. Patient-owned. And free — because managing a serious diagnosis is expensive enough.

🔓
Free. Always.
Live Every Day is free to download and free to use. Our ability to understand our own health and show up prepared to every appointment should not come with a subscription fee.
🔒
Our data. Our choice.
Our symptoms, our labs, our wearable patterns belong to us. We do not share data without explicit permission. If we choose to contribute de-identified data to research, we can — always opt-in, always reversible.
✓
HIPAA compliant.
Built to the same federal privacy framework that governs our medical records. Our health data is encrypted, protected, and handled with the same rigor our care team applies to our clinical information.
◎
Connected, not constrained.
We work alongside the healthcare system — connecting to labs, wearables, and patient portals — but our thinking is our own. Sitting closest to the people living it, and the people who love them.
